// bio

Jonah Oskow is a Los Angeles-based film and commercial editor. His work spans broadcast campaigns, viral social content, feature films, and emerging AI-driven formats.

Specializing in comedy, social media, episodic storytelling, and AI-directed content. A sharp sense of rhythm and an instinct for performance shape every cut — fast where it counts, patient where it matters.

He collaborates closely with directors, showrunners, and brand teams alike. Whether trimming a 90-minute cut or shaping a six-second social moment, the commitment to clarity and pace never wavers.

Clients range from major studios and streaming platforms to independent filmmakers and direct-to-consumer brands. Every project gets full attention and an edit that serves the story first.
