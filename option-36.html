About the Editor

Cutting stories that demand to be felt.

Jonah Oskow is a New York-based film and commercial editor whose work spans comedy, branded content, long-form narrative, and the emerging frontier of AI-directed media. With a precise instinct for rhythm and an obsessive attention to emotional truth, he shapes raw footage into work that resonates.

From network sketch comedy to independent features, Jonah brings the same disciplined craft and collaborative spirit to every project — whether the timeline is 30 seconds or 90 minutes.
