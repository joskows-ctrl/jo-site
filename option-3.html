About
The editor is the last writer. Every cut is an argument.

Jonah Oskow is a New York-based editor specializing in comedy, commercial, and long-form narrative work. With roots in NYC commercial editorial, his instinct for rhythm, timing, and performance has earned him recognition at the industry's most prestigious awards shows — Cannes Lions, Clios, and the One Show.

His long-form credits include work for Comedy Central and Netflix, with on-screen talent including Idris Elba, Pete Davidson, and Jamie Lee Curtis. Jonah operates at the intersection of high-craft comedy and emotionally resonant storytelling — finding the breath between the punchlines.

Clients span major advertising agencies, tech giants, and independent production companies. Jonah is available for commercial, episodic, and branded content projects.
