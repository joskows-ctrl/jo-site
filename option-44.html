Profile

Jonah Oskow

Jonah Oskow is a Los Angeles-based film and commercial editor whose work spans broadcast campaigns, viral social content, feature films, and emerging AI-driven formats. He brings a rare combination of technical precision and intuitive storytelling to every project he touches.

Specializing in comedy, social media, episodic storytelling, and AI-directed content, Jonah has developed a reputation for edits that move at exactly the right speed — fast where it counts, patient where it matters. His cuts feel inevitable in the best possible way.

He collaborates closely with directors, showrunners, and brand teams alike, treating each project as an opportunity to find the exact version of itself that will resonate most. Whether trimming a 90-minute cut or shaping a six-second social moment, the commitment to clarity and rhythm never wavers.

Clients range from major studios and streaming platforms to independent filmmakers and direct-to-consumer brands. Every project gets the same thing: full attention, sharp instincts, and an edit that serves the story first.
