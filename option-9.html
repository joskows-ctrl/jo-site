About

Jonah Oskow

A comedy editor with deep roots in NYC commercial editorial. Over a decade of shaping the rhythms, pauses, and punchlines that make audiences feel something — then make them laugh.

His credits span Comedy Central and Netflix, with on-screen collaborators including Idris Elba, Pete Davidson, and Jamie Lee Curtis. His advertising work has won at Cannes, the Clios, and the One Show. He cuts for major agencies and global technology companies.

The work is never accidental. Neither is the timing.
