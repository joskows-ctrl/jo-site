About Jonah Oskow

A career built on craft, timing, and the cut that changes everything.

Jonah Oskow is a New York-based film and commercial editor with a decade of work spanning comedy, branded content, narrative film, and the emerging world of AI-directed media. He approaches every timeline with the same rigorous attention to rhythm, pacing, and emotional truth.

From independent short films to network pilots to multi-platform social campaigns, Jonah brings a collaborative spirit and a disciplined editorial eye to projects of all scales and genres.
