About

A seasoned comedy editor with roots in NYC commercial editorial, Jonah Oskow has spent over a decade shaping the jokes, rhythms, and silences that make audiences lean in — then lose it. His reel spans broadcast comedy specials, award-winning campaigns, and narrative film, with credits at Comedy Central and Netflix.

On-screen collaborators include Idris Elba, Pete Davidson, and Jamie Lee Curtis. Clients range from major advertising agencies to global tech companies. The work has been recognized at Cannes, the Clios, and the One Show — because precision and wit are not mutually exclusive.
