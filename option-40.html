About the Editor

Craft Refined Through
Every Frame

Jonah Oskow is a New York-based film and commercial editor whose work spans comedy, branded content, long-form narrative, and the emerging frontier of AI-directed media. His editorial voice is built on rhythm, emotional precision, and a deep respect for the story in every cut.

Whether shaping a network comedy pilot or a single-take brand film, he brings the same obsessive craft to every project. The work is in the details — the frame you hold, the one you drop, and exactly when.
