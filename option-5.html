About

Seasoned comedy editor.
NYC commercial roots.

Jonah Oskow has spent his career at the intersection of comedy and craft — cutting spots that make people laugh, and episodes that make them stay. His editorial instincts are shaped by years in the fast-moving world of NYC advertising and sharpened by long-form narrative work for Comedy Central and Netflix.

He has collaborated with Idris Elba, Pete Davidson, and Jamie Lee Curtis, and built award-winning relationships with the industry's top agencies and tech brands. Cannes Lions, Clios, and One Show recognition reflect the standard he holds every frame to.
