About

Jonah Oskow

Jonah Oskow is a Los Angeles-based film and commercial editor with a specialization in comedy, social media content, episodic storytelling, and AI-directed projects. His work spans broadcast campaigns, viral social content, feature films, and emerging AI-driven formats.

With a sharp sense of rhythm and an instinct for performance, Jonah shapes raw footage into work that lands — whether the goal is a laugh, a gut punch, or a slow burn. He collaborates closely with directors and brands to find the edit that serves the story first.

Clients range from major studios and streaming platforms to independent filmmakers and direct-to-consumer brands. Every project gets the same commitment: clarity, pace, and intention.
