About

Jonah Oskow is a film and commercial editor based in New York. His comedy timing is precise, instinctive, and hard-won — built over years of cutting spots for major agencies and shaping performances for Comedy Central and Netflix originals.

Award-winning work includes Cannes Lions, Clio Awards, and One Show recognition. He has collaborated with Idris Elba, Pete Davidson, and Jamie Lee Curtis, among many others. Clients include leading advertising agencies and Fortune 500 technology companies.

Available for commercial & episodic work
