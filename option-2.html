About

Jonah Oskow is a New York-based film and commercial editor whose work spans network television, streaming originals, and the most-awarded ad campaigns of the past decade. His instinct for comedic timing is razor-sharp — equal parts craft and instinct, honed across hundreds of spots for the world's biggest brands.

He brings the same editorial eye to long-form work, having cut episodes for Comedy Central and Netflix originals. Working with talent like Idris Elba, Pete Davidson, and Jamie Lee Curtis, Jonah finds the human moment inside every take.

Clients include major advertising agencies and Fortune 500 tech companies. Emmy-nominated productions. International broadcast and streaming distribution.
